Jäger Bomb

Description

The Jägerbomb is a bomb mixed drink made by dropping a shot of Jägermeister into an energy drink, typically Red Bull. Sometimes, this drink is incorrectly identified as a traditional "shot".

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 ounces Jägermeister Liqueur
  • 1/2 (8.4-ounce) can Red Bull energy drink

Steps

  1. Fill a shot glass with Jägermeister.
  2. Pour half a can of Red Bull into a pint glass.
  3. Drop the shot glass into the taller glass.
